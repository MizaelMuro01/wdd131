Site Name

Site Name: Yonkett

Description: Yonkett is a modern platform for car profile management. The name means connection and technology, perfect for an app that connects users with smart car management.

Available Domain: yonkett.app

Site Purpose

Yonkett is a web platform that helps users create and manage multiple car profiles. It provides:

  • Central place for car information
  • Maintenance and cost tracking
  • Community for car owners to share experiences
  • Technical information and recommendations

Scenarios

Questions that website visitors might ask:

How does the platform work?

Users want to understand the registration process and main functions.

Why is there no marketplace?

Visitors might expect buy/sell functions and need to understand the platform's specific focus.

Can I have two or more car profiles?

Users with multiple vehicles need to manage them separately.

When can I have the complete app?

Users want to know the development roadmap and future features.

Color Schema

Dark Gray (#282828)

Used in: Headers, main text, and important elements

Red Accent (#FA0001)

Used in: Buttons, important actions, and alerts

Medium Gray (#4C4C4C)

Used in: Secondary text, borders, and backgrounds

White (#FFFFFF)

Used in: Main background and cards

Typography

Montserrat

Light 300: Used for secondary text and descriptions

Regular 400: Used for body text and main content

Medium 500: Used for navigation and buttons

Semibold 600: Used for subtitles

Bold 700: Used for main headings and titles
